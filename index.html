Quick Reference

  1. Boil kettle
  2. Fill basket — full, level, no tamp
  3. Fill boiler — to safety valve (light) or 2/3 (dark)
  4. Assemble with cloth, screw firmly
  5. Lowest flame on smallest burner
  6. Lid open — watch and listen
  7. Cut gas the moment liquid appears
  8. Cold tap immediately if sputtering
  9. Pour as soon as brewing finishes
